## Executive Summary

The personal data removal services market reached **$1.68 billion in 2024** and is projected to grow at **18.2% CAGR to $7.99 billion by 2033**. Despite 85% of American adults being concerned about data privacy, only **6% have used data removal services** — representing a massive market opportunity.

This report analyzes 8 major competitors, compares them to GhostMyData's current capabilities, identifies why competitors succeed, and provides an actionable improvement plan.

## Conclusion

GhostMyData has strong technical foundations with the **largest data broker directory (2,145+)** and **unique AI Shield protection**. However, to compete effectively, the focus should be on:

1. **Reducing friction** — Free trial, improved onboarding
2. **Building trust** — Third-party verification, transparency reports
3. **Improving engagement** — Mobile app, weekly reports, better dashboard
4. **Adding peace of mind** — Identity theft insurance
5. **Marketing differentiation** — Emphasize AI protection and broker coverage

The market is growing rapidly (18.2% CAGR), and with 94% of concerned adults not yet using these services, there's significant opportunity for a well-positioned player to capture market share.
