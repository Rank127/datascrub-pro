# Competitive Research - Data Removal Services (February 2026)

## Executive Summary

Most competitors use **custom enterprise pricing** rather than published per-seat rates. Only **Optery** has transparent business pricing. The market is shifting toward:
- Transparent pricing (Optery leading)
- Per-seat volume discounts
- SSO/SCIM as premium add-ons
- "Humans + Machines" hybrid approach for premium tiers

## Key Market Insights

### 1. Pricing Transparency Gap
- Only Optery publishes business pricing
- All others require "contact sales"
- **Opportunity:** Transparent pricing builds trust

### 2. SSO/SCIM Premium
- Optery charges $150/month extra for SSO on Core
- Most competitors include it in enterprise
- **Opportunity:** Include SSO in mid-tier to differentiate

### 3. Coverage Claims Vary
- Range: 150 (Kanary) to 980+ (Privacy Bee)
- Many inflate with "custom request" options
- **Opportunity:** Focus on quality over quantity

### 4. Volume Discount Standard
- 5-15% discounts for 50-100+ seats
- 20%+ for 500+ seats
- **Recommendation:** Match industry standard

### 5. Premium Add-ons Trend
- Executive protection
- Family coverage for employees
- Dark web monitoring
- API access
- **Recommendation:** Layer these into tiers
